Certified Tai Chi for Arthritis instructors
 

Tai Chi for Arthritis is a program designed by Dr Paul Lam and a group of medical and Tai Chi experts. It has gained worldwide acceptance by Arthritis Foundations and Societies and is proven by scientific studies to be safe and effective. Dr Lam and his authorised Master Trainers conduct global workshops to suitably qualified people.

 

Certified instructors must have attended a workshop, possess the suitable qualifications and fulfil the requirements including passing a written test, and be updated every two years. Instructors are trained to teach this program safely and effectively to people with or without arthritis. Click here for more information of the program. (please visit www.taichiforarthritis.com)
