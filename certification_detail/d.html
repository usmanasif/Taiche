Certified Tai Chi for Diabetes Instructors
 

Tai Chi for Diabetes is a program designed by Dr Paul Lam and a group of medical and Tai Chi experts. Supported by Diabetes Australia, this program is suitable for almost anyone but is especially designed for people with diabetes. It has taken into account the problems of people with diabetes and utilizing methodologies from both orthodox and Chinese traditional medicine to enhance its effect.

 

Certified instructors must have attended a workshop, possess the suitable qualifications and fulfill the requirements including passing a written test, and be updated every two years.
