Certified Tai Chi for Back Pain Instructor
 

This program is designed to relieve pain, restore the ability to work and play for people with back pain. It is also suitable for people who are wheelchair bound and with other chronic conditions to improve health and quality of life.

Based on the program Tai Chi for Arthritis, Dr Paul Lam and his team of medical and Tai Chi experts have integrated modern medicine findings with the ancient arts of tai chi to help people with back pain and other chronic conditions. He has added four Qigong exercises and incorporated the deep stabilizing muscles exercises of the back throughout the program.

 

Certified instructor of this program must be previously certified to instruct Tai Chi for Arthritis program to meet the more advanced requirements.
