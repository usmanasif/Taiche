Certified Tai Chi for Beginners instructors
 

In 1999, Dr Lam and tai chi experts from several Tai Chi schools composed a complete student -friendly program for practically all new students. This program has proved to attract new and retain regular students. Tai Chi for Beginners is based on the most popular Yang style 24 Forms Tai Chi. Starting with Step 1 warm up exercise, it progressively builds up to Step 6 the Beginners set, this gives students an easy to learn format that leads to a solid foundation. The program takes learners to on an enjoyable journey to better health and harmony through the power of Tai Chi.

 

Certified instructors must possess the suitable qualifications and fulfil the requirements including passing a written test, and update their certification every two years. Instructors are trained to teach this program safely and effectively.
